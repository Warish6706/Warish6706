Let's Know About Us

SVN Chaita was established in 2004 and it is managed by the Pvt. Aided. It is located in Rural area. It is located in Chaita (North), SAMASTIPUR district of Bihar. The school consists of Grades from pre to 10th. The school is Co-educational and it have an attached pre-primary section. The school is N/A in nature and is not using school building as a shift-school. Hindi & English are the medium of instructions in this school. This school is approachable by all weather road from Samastipur and Dalsingsarai. In this school academic session starts in April. The school has Private building. All the classrooms are in good condition. It has 10 other rooms for non-teaching activities. The school has a separate room for Principal, Teacher & other administrative system. The school has Pucca boundary wall. The school has a playground with full of sport equipments. The school does not need ramp for disabled children to access classrooms. The school has more than 20 computers for teaching and learning purposes and all are functional. All computer labs are well designed with latest technology.
